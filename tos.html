Animation Terms of Service

By commissioning me (DM'ing a brief, requesting work, or sending payment), you confirm you've read and agree to these terms. Breaches may result in the project being stopped and future work refused.

  1. Services & Scope

    Roblox animations with Moon Animator; I support R6 & R15 (primarily R6).
    I animate rigged models you provide. I do not rig models.
    I do not do viewmodels or dances.
    Pricing and timelines vary with complexity and quality of references.

  2. Pricing (reference ranges)

    M1 Pack: 1,000–2,000 R$ / $5–$20
    Simple / Movements: 1,000–2,000 R$ / $10–$20
    Skill / Ability (per): 10,000+ R$ / $25–$60
    Complex / Long (cutscenes): 15,000–50,000+ R$ / $35–$150

  3. Payments

    Robux — Group Payouts, T-shirt purchase, or Percentage (rev share).
    Crypto — Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, Solana.
    Percentage only for released & active games; terms must be agreed in writing before work starts.
    NO REFUNDS. All payments are final. Reasonable in-scope fixes are included (see Revisions).

  4. Revisions & Adjustments

    Included: up to two (2) minor revision rounds within the original brief.
    After delivery: reasonable fixes within the original scope are free of charge.
    Out-of-scope: new shots, major choreography, new rigs, or style changes are billed separately.

  5. Client Duties

    You own/have rights to all assets you provide (models, rigs, logos, audio, etc.).
    Reference and feedback are expected to be provided in a timely manner; delays can push delivery.

  6. Deliverables

    Final format(s) as agreed (Roblox animation assets, keyframes/clips, or preview render).
    Please back up your files after delivery; long-term storage on my side isn’t guaranteed.

  7. Usage & Restrictions

    License: after delivery you get a non-transferable license to use the animation inside your game/project.
    No resale/redistribution of the animation as a standalone asset/template.
    Credit “Altroll” is appreciated where reasonable (e.g., game credits/description).
    Your use must comply with Roblox/platform rules.

  8. Scheduling, Communication & Availability

    I work pay-per-task and may accept long-term work depending on project & rate.
    Timelines are estimates; complexity and feedback speed can shift delivery.
    If my message requests are flooded, ask a mutual to ping me to check them.

  9. Termination & Enforcement

    Breach of these terms may result in immediate termination and refusal of future commissions.
    On termination for breach, any license to use WIPs/finals may be revoked.

  10. Updates

    I may update this TOS; the version active when you commission applies to that project.

By commissioning me, you automatically agree to these terms.

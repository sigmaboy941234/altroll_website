Animation Terms of Service

By commissioning me (DMing a brief, requesting work, or sending payment), you confirm you have read and agree to these terms. Breaches may result in the project being stopped and future work refused.

  1. Services and Scope

    Roblox animations with Moon Animator; I support R6 and R15 (primarily R6).
    I animate rigged models you provide. I do not rig models.
    I do not do viewmodels or dances.
    Pricing and timelines vary with complexity and quality of references.

  2. Pricing (reference ranges)

    M1 Pack: 1,000-2,000 R$ / $5-$20
    Simple / Movements: 1,000-2,000 R$ / $10-$20
    Skill / Ability (per): 10,000+ R$ / $25-$60
    Complex / Long (cutscenes): 15,000-50,000+ R$ / $35-$150

  3. Payments

    Robux — Group Payouts, T-shirt purchase, or percentage (rev share).
    Crypto — Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, Solana.
    Percentage only for released and active games; terms must be agreed in writing before work starts.
    NO REFUNDS. All payments are final. Reasonable in-scope fixes are included (see Revisions).

  4. Revisions and Adjustments

    Included: up to two minor revision rounds within the original brief.
    After delivery: reasonable fixes within the original scope are free of charge.
    Out-of-scope: new shots, major choreography, new rigs, or style changes are billed separately.

  5. Client Duties

    You own or have rights to all assets you provide (models, rigs, logos, audio, etc.).
    Reference and feedback are expected to be provided in a timely manner; delays can push delivery.

  6. Deliverables

    Final formats as agreed (Roblox animation assets, keyframes/clips, or preview render).
    Please back up your files after delivery; long-term storage on my side is not guaranteed.

  7. Usage and Restrictions

    License: after delivery you get a non-transferable license to use the animation inside your game or project.
    No resale or redistribution of the animation as a standalone asset or template.
    Credit "Altroll" is appreciated where reasonable (for example game credits or description).
    Your use must comply with Roblox and platform rules.

  8. Scheduling, Communication, and Availability

    I work pay-per-task and may accept long-term work depending on project and rate.
    Timelines are estimates; complexity and feedback speed can shift delivery.
    If my message requests are flooded, ask a mutual to ping me to check them.

  9. Termination and Enforcement

    Breach of these terms may result in immediate termination and refusal of future commissions.
    On termination for breach, any license to use WIPs or finals may be revoked.

  10. Updates

    I may update this TOS; the version active when you commission applies to that project.

By commissioning me, you automatically agree to these terms.
